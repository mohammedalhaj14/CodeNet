## üìß Contact Integration Setup

To ensure the contact form works with your email:

1. Log in to your **Formspree** account.
2. Ensure the Form ID in `src/app/contact/page.tsx` matches your active form ID (`mykzroze`).
3. **Verification**: You must click the verification link in the email Formspree sends to `mohammedalhaj14@gmail.com` for the form to go live.
